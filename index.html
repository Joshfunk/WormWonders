Making the Most of Your Compost

In today's urban setting of smaller homes and condos, most people think composting is only for those with vast backyards and farms. Not true — composting can be done by everyone, from single-family homes with flower beds and raised vegetable plots to condo owners with wonderful balcony gardens.

So come on in… explore… learn… get your hands dirty… and go play in the garden…
